What is Sensor Jitter?

Jitter happens when a sensor cannot accurately track the surface, leading to "noisy" coordinates. Skipping happens when the sensor misses counts entirely during high-speed moves. High-quality sensors (PixArt 3360/3389/3395) should show a perfectly smooth line in the graph above.
