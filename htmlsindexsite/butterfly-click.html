What is Butterfly Clicking?

Butterfly clicking is a technique where you alternate between two fingers (usually index and middle) on a single mouse button. This effectively doubles your clicking potential, often reaching 15-25 CPS. It's highly popular in Minecraft PvP like Bridge or Bedwars.
