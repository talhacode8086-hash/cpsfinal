What is Pixel Skipping?

Pixel skipping occurs when the software multiplier (Windows or Game) is set high enough that 1 mouse count (dot) results in a movement of 2 or more pixels. This is generally undesirable for precise aim. Rule of thumb: Keep Windows at 6/11 and use higher DPI instead of higher in-game sens.
