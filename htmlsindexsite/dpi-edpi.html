Why eDPI Matters?

Different combinations of DPI and In-Game Sensitivity can lead to the same result. For example, 400 DPI with 2.0 Sensitivity is exactly the same as 800 DPI with 1.0 Sensitivity. eDPI allows you to compare your "true" sensitivity with other players regardless of their hardware settings.
