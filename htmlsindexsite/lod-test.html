Detect your mouse sensor's tracking cutoff elevation. Low LOD (< 2mm) is ideal for competitive play.

How to Test:

  1. Keep your mouse moving horizontally within the box.
  2. Slowly lift your mouse off the mousepad.
  3. The status will switch to RED the moment the sensor stops tracking.
  4. Repeat with CDs or playing cards stacked to measure the exact height in millimeters.
