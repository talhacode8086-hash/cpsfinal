What is Actuation Force?

It's the amount of pressure (measured in grams or centinewtons) required to depress a key and register a keystroke. Gamers typically prefer lighter switches (35g-45g) for speed, while typists may prefer heavier ones (55g-80g) to prevent accidental presses.
